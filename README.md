Free online video editor — no install, no signup, no watermark.

Trim, merge clips, add music and export to MP4 in 2 minutes, directly from your browser. No account required.

🔗 **[2minclip.com](https://2minclip.com/)**

## Features

- Upload multiple clips at once (MP4, MOV, WebM)
- Choose canvas format before editing: 9:16 (TikTok/Reels), 16:9 (YouTube), 1:1 (Instagram)
- Trim clips with precision using split + playhead
- Reorder clips by dragging
- Add audio tracks (MP3, AAC, WAV) with volume control and fade out
- Add text with custom fonts and colors
- Overlay images and videos
- Adjust clip speed (0.5x, 1x, 2x)
- Flip and rotate clips
- Export always as MP4 H.264 — no watermark, no limits

## How it works

Everything runs in your browser via FFmpeg.wasm. Your videos never leave your device — no server, no upload, no storage.

## Built with

- FFmpeg.wasm
